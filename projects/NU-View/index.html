The Northeastern University Office of Alumni Relations was looking for a creative way to stay engaged with their alumni and keep everyone updated with the goings on at the campus. Enter the NU View, a content aggregator and single stop for Northeastern Alumni to keep up with their alma mater.

Using Wordpress as a CMS allowed our developers to easily customize the REST API and utilize that in addition to a social aggregator to create a single news feed to populate the View. A NodeJS applicaiton running on the server rendered the application isomorphically for optimal SEO and performance.

Technology:

  • Webpack
  • SASS
  • React/Redux
  • NodeJS
  • Wordpress
