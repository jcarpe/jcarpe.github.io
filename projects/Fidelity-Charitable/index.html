Fidelity Chartiable is an organization that allows people very granular control over the distribution of funds to a huge number of charities. Fidelity wanted an improved experience both for users of their public facing site, as well as current users of their management tool and dashboard.

Working directly with the Fidelity technology team, we got to work creating a new single page Angular application for the logged in returning customer. Using Gulp, a custom static site generator was developed to meet the needs of the clients need to quickly and often update the content of their public facing site.

Technology:

  • Gulp & Broccoli
  • SASS
  • Angular 1.X
  • Karma
  • REST API for data

My roles:

  • Tech Track Team Lead on a geographically distributed team
  • Front End Developer
  • Technology liason to the client, working integrated with their own tech teams
