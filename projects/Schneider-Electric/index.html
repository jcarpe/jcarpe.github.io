Schneider Electric needed an eye catching installation for their world headquarters in Paris. Our proposed solution was over 20 feet of interactive screen! Built using web technologies with IR bezels for input, users could interact on a granular level with detailed content, or use the display as a presentation tool for a group.

The task of hardware selection also fell largely to the delopment team. Finding the proper hardware to drive so many screens well and handle such a large area of touch input was an extremely interesting challenge.

Technology:

  • Grunt
  • SASS
  • Backbone
  • NightwatchJS (testing)

My roles:

  • Technology Lead
  • Developer/Technologist
  • Technology liason to the client
  • On-site installation and testing
