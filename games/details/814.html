Army Men: Air Attack puts you in the cockpit of a miniature helicopter as you fly sorties for either the green or the tan army. With the new Captain William Blade leading the campaign, your objectives in the 16 different missions range from rescuing hostages, guarding trains, exterminating bugs, to just plain wiping out every enemy in sight. Four different plastic helicopters are at your disposal, each with machine gun, homing rockets, napalm, and everything else you would ever want for total annihilation. Have a friend join you in the co-op mode or the deathmatch mode to battle it out for air supremacy.
